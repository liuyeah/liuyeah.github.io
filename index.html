Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and a member of the Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application. My supervisor is Prof. Enhong Chen. I received my Bachelor degree from USTC in July, 2019, and majored in Electronic Information Engineering.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Graph and AI for Humanities (Law and Education). Recently I am focusing on Large Language Models (LLMs), including RLHF and LLM Applications.

I am looking for an internship in the above mentioned research area, please contact me if you are interested in me.
