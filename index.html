Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and a member of the Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application. My supervisor is Prof. Enhong Chen. I received my Bachelor degree from USTC in July, 2019, and majored in Electronic Information Engineering.

My research interests include Natural Language Processing, Knowledge Graph and Large Language Models (LLMs).

Currently, I am a visiting PhD student with Prof. Xiaofang Zhou at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

I am on the job market the next year! I will graduate with my PhD degree in spring 2025. I am open to faculty/postdoctoral positions or related industry roles. Please get in touch!
